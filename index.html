How to play?


1. Click on a tile to reveal its color.
2. Guess the tile with same color.
a. If right, marks increase by 25.
b. If wrong, marks decrease by 5.
3. The game is played till all colors are revealed.


Note: 1. Colors are in RGB code.
2. No more than two colors are same.
